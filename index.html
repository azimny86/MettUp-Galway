MettUp-Galway

We organize meetings for specialists in the IT department of the city of Galway. You can come to our meeting at any level of advancement.Our events are run by specialists with a long-term experience in programming.We are a developer team specializing in a wide range of technologies. The only thing we have in common is that we are always ready to learn something new. This is what our meetings are all about - in pursuit of the newest way to better serve people. Staying true to our authenticity is important to us because through this authenticity we have built our common unity, represented by a space where we can grow together and grow as individuals and reflect this in our code, our way of working and our way of life
